How to Add Items to Inventory

To add items to your inventory, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the "Add New Item" section in the inventory management interface.
  2. Fill in the item details, including the name, quantity, price, and category.
  3. Click the "Add Item" button to save the item to your inventory.
  4. You will see a confirmation message indicating that the item has been added successfully.

How to Update Existing Items

To update existing items in your inventory, follow these steps:

  1. Locate the item you wish to update in the inventory list.
  2. Click the "Edit" button next to the item.
  3. Modify the item details as needed, including name, quantity, price, and category.
  4. Click the "Save" button to apply the changes.
  5. You will see a confirmation message indicating that the item has been updated successfully.

How to Delete Items from Inventory

To delete items from your inventory, follow these steps:

  1. Locate the item you wish to delete in the inventory list.
  2. Click the "Delete" button next to the item.
  3. A confirmation prompt will appear. Confirm that you want to delete the item.
  4. The item will be removed from your inventory, and you will see a confirmation message.

How to Search for Items

To search for items in your inventory, follow these steps:

  1. Enter the item name or keyword in the search bar located at the top of the inventory page.
  2. Click the "Search" button.
  3. The inventory list will be filtered to show only the items that match your search criteria.

How to Filter Inventory

To filter your inventory based on specific criteria, follow these steps:

  1. Use the filter options available, such as quantity or price range.
  2. Enter the desired minimum and maximum values for the filter.
  3. Click the "Filter" button to apply the filters.
  4. The inventory list will update to show only the items that meet your filter criteria.

Using Barcode Scanning

To add or update items using barcode scanning, follow these steps:

  1. Scan the barcode of the item using a barcode scanner.
  2. If the item exists, you will be prompted to update the quantity.
  3. If the item does not exist, you will be prompted to enter the item details such as name, price, and category.
  4. Follow the prompts to complete the action.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Follow the instructions to reset it.

2. Can I import items from a CSV file?

Yes, you can import items from a CSV file by clicking on the "Import CSV" button in the inventory section.

3. What should I do if I encounter an error?

If you encounter an error, please check the input fields for any mistakes. If the problem persists, contact support.
